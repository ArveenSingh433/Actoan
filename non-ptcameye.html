Product 03

Non-Pt Cam: Wireless CCTV Security Camera for Home

The security of your loved ones and your home matters like nothing else. To ensure complete safety, bring home our new age AI-based Smart Home Camera that offers enhanced security with round-the-clock recording and state-of-the-art features.

Key Features

  • Camera Clarity: 2MP (1080P) Resolution ensures incredibly clear video recording with live video and playback.
  • Full Offline Recording: Continuous recording even if Wi-Fi disconnects or during power fluctuations. Automatically restarts seamlessly.
  • Live Video Streaming: Excellent quality live video streaming for real-time monitoring.
  • Two-Way Calling: Effortless communication with your family at home at the touch of a button.
  • True Cloud Camera: Event-based cloud recording for efficient data storage.
  • Night Vision: Clear visibility even during nighttime with six invisible infrared LEDs.
  • AI Motion Detection: Real-time alerts on your phone when motion is detected within the activity zone.
  • 106° FOV: Wide field of view to cover maximum area in your house or shop.
  • Continuous Recording (24x7): Non-stop video recording on a microSD card (up to 128 GB), offering up to 60 days of footage in low quality.
  • Multiple Mounting Options: Mount it on a tabletop or wall for your convenience.
