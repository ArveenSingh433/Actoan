Internet of Things(IoT)

IoT is no more a new buzz-word in the market but different people have different perspective to deal with. Technically speaking its the network of physical devices, vehicles, home appliances and other items embedded with electronics, software, sensors, actuators, and connectivity which enables these objects to connect and exchange data.
