Cam 360 - Next-Gen Home Security Camera

Security of your home and family comes first. Begin this uncompromising journey of safety with the next-gen Wi-Fi camera – sweeps 360° area and packs smart AI-based alerts.

What Makes CAM 360 Unique?

Fully equipped to help you safeguard your home and family from potential dangers.

Features:

  • 360° Panoramic View: 2MP (1080P) Resolution: Ensure incredibly clear video recording with live video and playback. Ensure peace of mind with 360° Coverage, 24x7 Live Monitoring, Continuous Recording.
  • Intruder Alarm: Loud audible alarm on Person Detection.
  • Motion Tracking: Infrared LEDs rotate automatically to follow a moving person or object on motion, receive instant alerts for motion or people detected, enabling you to take action and prevent potential threats.
  • Night Vision: Crystal-clear recording up to 9.15 meters (30 feet) using invisible IR LED.
  • 360° Coverage: Its motor head allows a full 360° horizontal view, with pan and tilt control through the app for customizable coverage.
  • Two-Way Communication: Built-in microphone and speaker facilitate communication with pets and family in superior video quality from anywhere.
  • Multiple Mounting Options: Mount it any way you want, whether on a tabletop or on a wall, for your convenience.
  • Voice Control with Alexa: Voice control your smart security cams remotely, changing angles of the camera easily for monitoring of your home.
  • SD Card Support: Up to 128GB.
